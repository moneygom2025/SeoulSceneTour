# Privacy Policy for Seoul Scene Hunter
**Last updated: November 30, 2025**

Seoul Scene Hunter (“the App”, “we”, or “us”) is a travel guide application designed to help users explore landmarks, filming locations, and recommended routes around Seoul. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

We value your privacy and aim to collect **no personal data** whenever possible.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

Seoul Scene Hunter **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable information.

The App does *not* collect:
- Name, email, phone number, or address  
- Account or login information  
- Photos, media, or files  
- Device identifiers  
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)  
- Analytics or usage data  
- Search or browsing history  
- Location history  
- Payment information  

The App does not use tracking technologies and does not connect to external servers to gather user data.

---

## 2. Location Information

The App uses your device’s location **only to display nearby landmarks and enable navigation features**.

Important:
- Your location is used **in real time only**  
- Your location is **not stored**  
- Your location is **never transmitted** to servers  
- Your location is **not used for tracking or analytics**  

You may disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.  
The App will continue to work with limited features.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Seoul Scene Hunter does **not** use any third-party services such as:
- Analytics platforms  
- Advertising networks  
- Cloud storage  
- Social login services  
- External databases  

The App uses only Apple-provided frameworks (e.g., MapKit, CoreLocation), which operate under Apple's strict privacy guidelines.

---

## 4. Local Data Stored on the Device

The App may store the following **non-personal** data locally:
- App settings (language, region filters, UI preferences)  
- Temporary route or map view selections  

This data:
- Remains solely on your device  
- Is not transmitted externally  
- Is automatically removed when the App is deleted  

No personal or sensitive data is included.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from users, including children under 13.  
Seoul Scene Hunter is safe for users of all ages.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Because the App does not collect or store personal data:
- There is no personal data to access or request  
- No data needs correction or deletion  
- No account information is stored  

Deleting the App removes all locally stored settings.

---

## 7. Data Security

Although no personal data is collected, we follow standard security practices to ensure that App functionality remains safe and compliant.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed due to legal requirements or App improvements.  
Any updates will be reflected in the “Last updated” date at the top of this document.
